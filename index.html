Our Goals

FASTER & AMICABLY RESOLUTION,QUICK RESPONSE

1

To develop, promote, facilitate, and otherwise engage in the Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) mechanism methods, program, expert advice, consultancy, recruiting, guidance, support and related services and to act as an entity for promoting, encouraging, and providing ADR services.

2

To substitute, administer, manage and provide alternatives to conventional means of resolving disputes, including civil, commercial, industrial, tax, finance and family matters, through mechanisms such as Arbitration, Reconciliation, Mediation, Negotiation, Lok-Adalat, and other ADR methods.

3

To collaborate with individuals, organizations, governmental bodies, and institutions, both national and international, for the promotion and development of ADR mechanisms.

4

To carry on the Activity like organizing seminars, workshops, awareness camps, conference, Counseling and training program both online and offline for Promoting, Imparting, Launching, Creating, Training, Adopting, promoting and development of ADR mechanisms, amongst the masses of India and Abroad.

5

To extend ADR services to the entire territory of India and establish offices, branches, or centers in various regions for the efficient functioning of ADR processes.
